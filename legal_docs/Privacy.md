PRIVACY NOTICE

Welcome to Kojori Bus Time App (“App”, “We”, “Us”, “Our”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Notice describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our Services.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We may collect the following types of information:

(a) Personal Information you provide directly:
- None required for basic app functionality
- Contact information only if you choose to provide it

(b) Information automatically collected:
- Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
- IP address
- Usage data (features accessed, routes viewed, timestamps)
- Location information (only if you enable location services for transit features)
- Cookies and other tracking technologies

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We use your information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Services
- Send you relevant transit information and updates
- Diagnose and troubleshoot technical issues
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
- Enable location-based features (route suggestions, nearest stops)
- Send you important notices about service changes or disruptions

3. LOCATION DATA
Location services are optional and used only to:
- Suggest the nearest bus stops based on your current location
- Provide automatic direction switching (Kojori ↔ Tbilisi)
- Enhance route planning accuracy

You can disable location access at any time through your device settings without affecting core functionality.

4. DATA SHARING
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes, except:
- With your explicit consent
- To service providers who assist us in operating the Services (e.g., analytics, hosting)
- When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

5. DATA RETENTION
We retain your information:
- As long as your app usage requires it
- For a reasonable period for record-keeping and legal compliance
- As required by applicable laws and regulations

6. YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing (if applicable in your jurisdiction)
- Opt-out of certain data collection practices where permitted

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [Your Contact Information]

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES
We use cookies and similar technologies to:
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Analyze traffic and usage patterns
- Enhance security and functionality

You can configure your browser to reject or alert you about cookies, though some features may not work properly.

8. SECURITY
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

9. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.
