# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Kojori Bus Time ("the App") is a free, open-source transit information app for bus routes 380 and 316 between Tbilisi and Kojori, Georgia.

## 1. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not have servers — the App communicates directly with the TTC (Tbilisi Transport Company) public API.

## 2. Location Data

When you grant permission, the App accesses your device location solely to:

- Show your position on the map
- Auto-detect your travel direction (Tbilisi or Kojori)

Location data is processed on-device only and is never transmitted to any third party beyond what the operating system provides.

## 3. Local Storage

The App caches bus schedules, stop names, and route data on your device using AsyncStorage for offline use. This data contains no personal information and can be cleared from Settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services

- **TTC API**: The App fetches real-time bus positions and schedules from the TTC public transit API. These requests contain no personally identifiable information.
- **Google Maps SDK**: Used to render the map. Google's own privacy policy applies to map tile loading.

## 5. Analytics & Advertising

The App contains no analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry of any kind.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the App update and the "Last updated" date above.
